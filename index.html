Introduction

We are a pioneering online therapeutic service designed exclusively for women. At Empower, we understand the unique emotional and psychological challenges women face throughout different stages of their lives.

Our mission is to offer accessible, specialized clinical psychology support, providing a seamless and convenient way to access the care and guidance needed to navigate life's complexities. Through our platform, women can easily connect with experienced therapists, enjoy the privacy of online sessions, and find a path to empowerment and improved mental well-being, all from the comfort of their own homes. Empower is committed to fostering a supportive environment that encourages healing and growth, ensuring every woman feels heard, understood, and valued.

Dr. Christina Geary

DClin, PhD.

Dr Christina Geary is a Chartered Clinical Psychologist with a rich educational background, including a Doctorate in Clinical Psychology from University College Cork and both a BSc and a PhD in Psychology from Ulster University.

Her distinguished research focuses on the menopause and the digitalisation of mental health services, underscoring her commitment to women's health and innovative therapy solutions. Christina's therapeutic approach is notably integrative, utilizing a wide spectrum of modalities such as CBT, DBT, Psychodynamic therapy, and Mindfulness, among others.

This versatility enables her to tailor therapy to the individual needs of her clients, fostering a deep, empathetic, and non-judgmental therapeutic relationship. Christina's primary aim in therapy is to cultivate a strong connection with her clients, offering a supportive space for exploration and growth.

What is online therapy?

Online therapy offers psychological support through digital platforms, allowing clients to connect with registered therapists from any location. It includes individual and group sessions conducted via secure video conferencing.

How does Empower ensure privacy in online sessions?

Empower utilizes encrypted video conferencing tools to maintain confidentiality. All communications and records are securely stored, adhering to strict privacy laws and ethical guidelines.

What can I expect from my first session?

Your first session involves discussing your needs and goals, understanding the therapy process, and starting to build a rapport with your therapist. It's a space to ask questions and set expectations.

How can I book a session?

Sessions can be booked directly through our website by selecting the desired service, choosing a convenient time, and filling out a brief pre-session form.

Are corporate workshops and talks available for workplace support?

Yes, Empower offers tailored workshops and talks for organizations seeking to support women's mental health and well-being in the workplace. Contact us for more details and scheduling.
